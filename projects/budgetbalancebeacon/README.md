# BudgetBalanceBeacon

A simple command-line tool to calculate the impact of a new expense on your budget.

## Usage

Run `python main.py` and follow the prompts to enter your monthly income, current expenses, and a new expense. The program will calculate your current budget balance and the new balance after adding the expense.

## Example

```
Monthly Income: 3000
Current Expenses: 2500
New Expense: 200

Your current budget balance is: $500.00
Your budget balance after the new expense is: $300.00
```
